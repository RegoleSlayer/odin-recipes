Baked Chicken Breast

An air fryer recipe for seasoning and cooking delicious chicken breasts!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken Breast
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Place the chicken breasts on a cutting board and flatten them. Cut the chicken breast in half, if desired.
  2. Place in a bowl and sprinkle with salt, then place the bowl in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to brine.
  3. While the chicken is brining, preheat the air fryer at 375 degrees.
  4. Gather your seasoning ingredients and mix them together.
    • 1 tablespoon Paprika
    • 2 teaspoons Chili Powder
    • 2 teaspoons Oregano
    • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
    • 1 teaspoon Salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon Onion Powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
  5. Remove the chicken breast from the fridge once it is done brining. Coat in olive oil.
  6. Use 1 tablespoon of seasoning mix per pound of chicken and cover the surface area of the chicken as much as possible.
  7. Place them in the pre-heated air fryer and let them cook for 20 minutes, flipping them over after 10 minutes.
  8. Use a meat thermometer to check their temperature once the timer has finished, they should be 165 degrees when they are ready.
